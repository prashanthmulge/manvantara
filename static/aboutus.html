BRIEF REPORT OF ORGANIZATION

Manvantara is a Non Government Organization started in the year 2012 It has been registered under the following Government Departments:

  • Karnataka Society Act.
  • 12A & 80 G, PAN & TAN under Income Tax Act
  • FCRA (Foreign Contribution Regulation Act.

Activities:

  • Lift Irrigation Schemes
  • Self Help Groups
  • Education
  • Tank Development
  • NREGA

LIFT IRRIGATION SCHEMES: This is the first project started from our Organization on the bank of river Krishna & Bheema at Raichur & Gulbarga Districts. We have given awareness to farmers that how to lift water from river and to be utilized for their field. We have done this project by the support of SC/ST Corporation & BCM Corporation. Nearly 300 schemes has been made from the both districts. The main object is that dry land is to be converted into irrigated.

SELF HELP GROUPS: This project is on women empowerment. In villages women are earning but they don’t know about how to save the money, how the money is to be turnover and what kind of income generation is to be make. Regarding this we have given awareness to village people . We have made women groups in villages by taking 20 women in each group. After 06 months those groups is to make bank linkages. Near about 600 groups are made in three districts, two from Karnataka & one from Andhra Pradesh. i.e., Raichur, Gulbarga & Mahabubnagar.The donor for this project is Dorabji Tata Trust, Mumbai.

EDUCATION: This is the third project started from our Organization in the year 2001-02. Under this project we have supported scholarship to meritorious with economically weak students after 10th standard. In the year 2001-02 we have supported only 32 students an amount of Rs. 28000 in Raichur Local. This project has been extended to Six districts. Now we are supporting scholarship to Professional students since 2011-12 i.e., to Engineer,Medical, B.Sc Agri & Nursing Courses. This project has been done by three NGO’s in Six Districts –Raichur ,Gulbarga ,Bellary,Yadgiri Koppal & Bidar Districts. TANK DEVELOPMENT: This project started in the year 2004-05 & concluded in the year 2007-08. It is a State Government Project. In this project we worked as anchor NGO. The main aspect of this project is in villages where-ever tanks are that can be utilized for irrigation purpose . The major work is removing mud from the tank and constructed bunds to tank. This project completed in Raichur,Koppal & Bidar Districts.

NREGA: This stands for National Rural Employment Guarantee Act. This project started from our Organizaion from 1st May,2011 ,it is two years project concluding on 31st May,3013. The object of this project is giving 100 days coolie to each family in villages. The payment for one day is Rs.145/- Now it has been enhanced from Rs.145/- to 174/-

In this project we have made groups of families by minimum 25 and maximum to 30 in each group. And also we will collect Rs.10/- as registration fees & Rs.100/- as membership . In these two years we have made around 307 groups and collected Rs.388000/- The donor for this project is Sir Dorabji Tata Trust,Mumbai.
